About webpng

A free, private PNG ↔ WebP converter that runs entirely in your browser.

webpng uses HTML5 Canvas to convert images on your device. Files are not uploaded to any server. This makes it fast and privacy-friendly, especially for images with transparency.

Why WebP?

WebP usually delivers dramatically smaller files than PNG or JPEG while keeping visual quality. That means faster page loads and lower bandwidth.

Features

  • PNG → WebP and WebP → PNG
  • Quality control for WebP output
  • Preserves transparency
  • Works offline after first load (static page)
